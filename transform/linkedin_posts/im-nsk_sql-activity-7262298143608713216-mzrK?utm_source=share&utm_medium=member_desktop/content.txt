Write a query that compares each employee's salary to their manager's and the average department salary (excluding the manager's salary). Display the department, employee ID, employee's salary, manager's salary, and department average salary. Order by department, then by employee salary (highest to lowest).

1. DepartmentAvgSalary CTE: This calculates the average salary for each department, excluding the department manager’s salary by checking id != manager_id.

2. ManagerSalary CTE: This gets each employee's salary along with their manager’s salary using a self-join on the manager_id column, but excludes department managers who report to themselves.

3. Final Select Statement: This combines the employee’s details, their manager’s salary, and the department’s average salary. We use ORDER BY to list employees in each department by their salary in descending order.
